Kaiko Rafting Co.

Our mission is to connect people with nature by offering safe, thrilling, and unforgettable rafting adventures. We believe that conquering river rapids is not only fun, but it also renews the spirit and creates lifelong friendships. Join us and experience pure adrenaline!

History

Kaiko Rafting Co. was founded in 2010 by a small group of whitewater enthusiasts who wanted to share their love for the outdoors. We started with just a couple of rafts and an unwavering passion for exploration. Thanks to our commitment to safety and fun, we have grown to become one of the leading rafting experience providers in the region, featuring the best professional guides in the country.
